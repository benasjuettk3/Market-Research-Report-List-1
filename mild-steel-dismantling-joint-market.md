<p><p>The Mild Steel Dismantling Joint market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Sphere and Double Sphere joints. Single Sphere joints feature a single flexible connection, allowing for easier alignment and limited movement, making them suitable for straightforward applications. In contrast, Double Sphere joints offer enhanced flexibility and movement in multiple directions, accommodating more complex piping arrangements and minimizing stress on connected components. Both types serve critical roles in facilitating maintenance and expansion in various industrial piping systems.</p></p>
