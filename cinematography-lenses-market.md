<p><ul><li>Prime Lenses</li><li>Zoom Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cinematography lenses market is primarily divided into two types: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses feature a fixed focal length, offering superior image quality, wider apertures, and enhanced low-light performance, making them ideal for artistic shots. In contrast, zoom lenses provide variable focal lengths, allowing filmmakers greater flexibility in composition without changing lenses. While prime lenses are favored for their optical excellence, zoom lenses are popular for convenience and versatility in dynamic shooting environments, catering to diverse filmmaking needs.</p></p>
