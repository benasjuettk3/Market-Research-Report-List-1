<p><p>Narrow Band IoT (NB-IoT) operates in three market types: In-Band, Guard Band, and Standalone. In-Band utilizes existing LTE spectrum, integrating IoT with traditional mobile networks. Guard Band operates within a specified range between two LTE frequencies, minimizing interference and maximizing resource efficiency. Standalone serves dedicated low-frequency bands for IoT, enabling operators to deploy NB-IoT without relying on existing networks. Each type addresses different deployment scenarios and spectrum availability, catering to diverse IoT applications and market needs.</p></p>
