<p><p>The flat drop cable market includes two primary types: single-mode and multi-mode cables. Single-mode cables, designed for long-distance communication, utilize a single light path to transmit data, ensuring minimal signal loss and higher bandwidth over extended ranges. In contrast, multi-mode cables support shorter distances and use multiple light paths, making them suitable for local area networks and data centers. Both types are essential for modern telecommunications, providing reliable connectivity in various applications, from residential to commercial settings.</p></p>
