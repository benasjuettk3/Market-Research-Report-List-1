<p><p>The Rotor Centrifugal Crusher market includes three main types based on the crushing mechanism: </p><p>1. **Rock on Rock Rotor Centrifugal Crusher**, where materials are crushed against each other, enhancing the production of sand and reducing wear on the machine. </p><p>2. **Rock on Iron Rotor Centrifugal Crusher**, which utilizes hard metal anvils, providing efficient crushing while producing a finer material. </p><p>3. **Rock on Anvil Rotor Centrifugal Crusher**, combining both rock and anvil interactions for improved shape and quality of the final product. Each type serves specific applications in material processing and recycling.</p></p>
