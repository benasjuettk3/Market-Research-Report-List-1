<p><ul><li>Non-Continuously</li><li>Continuously With External Measurements</li><li>Continuously With Internal Measurements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water Pipeline Leak Detection Systems (LDS) are crucial for monitoring and identifying leaks in water distribution networks. The market is segmented into three types: </p><p>1. Non-Continuously: These systems perform periodic checks and rely on occasional inspections to detect leaks.  </p><p>2. Continuously With External Measurements: These utilize external sensors to monitor pipeline conditions continuously for real-time leak detection.  </p><p>3. Continuously With Internal Measurements: These involve internal sensors placed within the pipeline, providing constant monitoring of flow and pressure to ensure immediate leak identification.</p></p>
