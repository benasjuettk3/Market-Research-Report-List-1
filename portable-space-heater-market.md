<p><strong>The Portable Space Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convector Heater</li><li>Radiant Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable space heater market consists mainly of two types: convector heaters and radiant heaters. Convector heaters operate by warming air that circulates through the unit, providing even and gentle heat throughout a room, making them ideal for consistent heating. In contrast, radiant heaters emit infrared heat, directly warming objects and people in their vicinity, which offers immediate warmth but may leave surrounding air cooler. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences for heating efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
