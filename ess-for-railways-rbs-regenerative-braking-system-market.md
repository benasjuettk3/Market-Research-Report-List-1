<p><p>The Energy Storage System (ESS) for Railways focuses on enhancing regenerative braking systems (RBS) through both wayside and onboard configurations. The wayside system captures and stores energy generated during braking, redistributing it to the rail network for future use. Conversely, onboard systems store energy within the train itself, allowing for immediate reuse during subsequent acceleration. Both approaches improve energy efficiency, reduce operational costs, and minimize environmental impact by harnessing energy that would normally be wasted during braking processes.</p></p>
