<p><p>The Smart Cards Automated Fare Collection System encompasses three main market types: Fareboxes, Ticket Vending Machines (TVMs), and Validators. Fareboxes facilitate on-board fare collection, allowing passengers to deposit smart cards or cash. TVMs enable users to purchase tickets or load smart cards at kiosks, enhancing convenience and reducing queues. Validators are installed at entry and exit points, verifying smart card usage, ensuring accurate fare deductions, and seamless passenger flow. Together, these components optimize the fare collection process in public transit.</p></p>
