<p><strong>The AC Tachometer Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Induction Tachogenerators</li><li>Synchronous Tachogenerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC tachometer generators are devices that generate electrical output proportional to rotational speed, commonly used in industrial applications. There are two primary types: induction tachogenerators and synchronous tachogenerators. Induction tachogenerators rely on electromagnetic induction and are known for their ruggedness and simplicity, making them ideal for harsh environments. In contrast, synchronous tachogenerators provide higher accuracy and stability, utilizing magnetic synchronization with the input speed. Both types play crucial roles in speed measurement and control in various industrial processes.</p></p>
