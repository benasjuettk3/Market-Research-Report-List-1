<p><ul><li>Double Coated Tape</li><li>Single Coated Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum tape market features two primary types: double coated tape and single coated tape. Double coated tape has adhesive on both sides, providing strong bonding for various applications, including insulation and construction. It is ideal for creating seamless joints and enhancing thermal performance. In contrast, single coated tape has adhesive on one side, making it suitable for general-purpose sealing and repairing tasks. Both types are valued for their durability, moisture resistance, and effectiveness in diverse industries.</p></p>
