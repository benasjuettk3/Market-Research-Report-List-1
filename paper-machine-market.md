<p><p>The paper machine market consists of various types of equipment essential for pulp and paper production. Continuous digesters process wood chips into pulp, while pulp washers remove impurities. Refiners enhance pulp quality, and black liquor recovery boilers recycle chemicals. Bleaching towers whiten pulp, and Fourdrinier machines form sheets of paper. Chip piles store raw materials, while conveyors facilitate movement throughout the processing stages. Together, these components optimize efficiency and quality in the paper manufacturing process.</p></p>
