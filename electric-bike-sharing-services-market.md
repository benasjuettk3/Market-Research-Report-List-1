<p><p>Electric bike sharing services can be categorized into two main market types: dockless and station-based systems. Dockless bike sharing allows users to pick up and drop off bikes at any location within a designated area, offering greater flexibility and convenience. In contrast, station-based systems require users to rent bikes from specific stations and return them to the same or another designated docking station. Both models aim to promote sustainable transportation, reduce congestion, and enhance urban mobility.</p></p>
