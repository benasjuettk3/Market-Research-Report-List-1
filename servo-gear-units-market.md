<p><p>The servo gear units market comprises several types, primarily linear servo gearboxes and right angle servo gearboxes. Linear servo gearboxes convert rotary motion into linear movement, facilitating precise positioning in applications like robotics and automation. In contrast, right angle servo gearboxes enable the transfer of motion at a 90-degree angle, making them ideal for space-constrained environments and complex machinery setups. Both types enhance operational efficiency and accuracy, benefiting various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
