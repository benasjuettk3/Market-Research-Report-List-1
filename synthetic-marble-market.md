<p><strong>The Synthetic Marble Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cement Synthetic Marble</li><li>Polyester Synthetic Marble</li><li>Composite Synthetic Marble</li><li>Sintered Synthetic Marble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic marble market comprises four key types: </p><p>1. **Cement Synthetic Marble** is made from a blend of cement and marble dust, offering durability and versatility.</p><p>2. **Polyester Synthetic Marble** utilizes polyester resins, providing a polished finish and diverse colors.</p><p>3. **Composite Synthetic Marble** combines natural stone particles with resins for enhanced strength and aesthetics.</p><p>4. **Sintered Synthetic Marble** involves high-temperature processing, resulting in a dense, robust material suitable for various applications. Each type caters to different preferences and usage requirements.</p></p>
