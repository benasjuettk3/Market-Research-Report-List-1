<p><ul><li>Spatial Scanning Sensors</li><li>Spectral Scanning Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical imaging sensor market encompasses two primary types: spatial scanning sensors and spectral scanning sensors. Spatial scanning sensors capture images based on the spatial arrangement of tissues and structures, providing high-resolution anatomical details for diagnosis. In contrast, spectral scanning sensors analyze multiple wavelengths of light, enabling the detection of various tissue characteristics and chemical compositions, facilitating advanced imaging techniques like MRI and PET scans. Both sensor types enhance diagnostic accuracy and are essential in modern medical imaging technologies.</p></p>
