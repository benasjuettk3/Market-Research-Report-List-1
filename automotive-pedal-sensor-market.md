<p><ul><li>Brake Pedal Sensor</li><li>Accelerator Pedal Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive pedal sensor market is primarily divided into two main types: brake pedal sensors and accelerator pedal sensors. Brake pedal sensors detect the position and force applied on the brake pedal, enabling efficient braking performance and enhancing safety features like anti-lock braking systems (ABS). Conversely, accelerator pedal sensors monitor the position of the accelerator pedal, allowing for precise throttle control, improved fuel efficiency, and optimized engine performance. Both sensors play a critical role in the overall vehicle dynamics and driver experience.</p></p>
