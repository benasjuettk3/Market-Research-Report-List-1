<p><p>The Greek crumbles market is categorized into three types based on the firmness of the cheese: soft, semi-hard, and hard. </p><p>Soft crumbles, like feta, are creamy, easily spreadable, and often used in salads or as a topping. Semi-hard crumbles, such as graviera, have a firmer texture and are versatile for cooking and snacking.
