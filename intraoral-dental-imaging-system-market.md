<p><strong>The Intraoral Dental Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bite Wing X-Rays</li><li>Apical X-Rays</li><li>Occlusal X-Ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraoral dental imaging system market includes various types of X-ray techniques, such as bite wing X-rays, apical X-rays, and occlusal X-rays. Bite wing X-rays are used to detect interproximal caries and assess existing restorations. Apical X-rays focus on the apices of teeth to identify periapical diseases and root issues. Occlusal X-rays capture the entire arch, providing a comprehensive view for detecting abnormalities in tooth alignment and bone structure. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes in dental care.</p></p>
