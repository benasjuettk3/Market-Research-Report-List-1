<p><ul><li>Structural Imaging</li><li>Functional Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro-CT market is divided into two main types: structural imaging and functional imaging. Structural imaging focuses on capturing detailed 3D representations of the anatomical features of samples, enabling precise visualization of their internal architecture. Conversely, functional imaging assesses physiological processes, providing insights into the metabolic and chemical activities occurring within tissues or organisms. Both types serve distinct purposes in research and clinical applications, aiding in studies ranging from material science to biomedical research, ultimately enhancing our understanding of complex systems.</p></p>
