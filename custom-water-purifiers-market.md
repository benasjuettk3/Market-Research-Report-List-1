<p><ul><li>RO Water Purifier</li><li>UV Water Purifier</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom water purifiers market is categorized primarily into three types: RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers, UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers, and others. RO water purifiers work by removing impurities and contaminants through a semipermeable membrane, making them effective for hard water sources. UV water purifiers use ultraviolet light to disinfect water by killing bacteria and viruses, ensuring microbiological safety. The "Others" category includes alternatives like activated carbon filters and sediment filters, catering to specific purification needs and preferences.</p></p>
