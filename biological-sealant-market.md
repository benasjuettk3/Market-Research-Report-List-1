<p><p>The biological sealant market encompasses various types, primarily classified into fibrin-based, collagen-based, and other categories. Fibrin sealants, derived from blood components, promote wound healing and tissue adhesion, making them suitable for surgical applications. Collagen-based sealants utilize natural collagen to enhance tissue repair and regeneration, benefiting orthopedic and dental procedures. Other sealants may include synthetic or plant-based options, broadening the market's applications. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific medical needs, improving surgical outcomes and patient recovery.</p></p>
