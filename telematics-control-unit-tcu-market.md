<p><p>A Telematics Control Unit (TCU) is an essential device in vehicles that facilitates communication between the vehicle and external services, enabling a range of applications such as navigation, vehicle tracking, and emergency assistance. TCUs gather data from various sensors within the vehicle and leverage cellular, GPS, or satellite communication systems to transmit this information to automotive manufacturers, service centers, or directly to consumers.</p><p>The TCU market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the rising demand for advanced connectivity features in vehicles, increasing consumer preferences for safety and convenience, and the growing trend toward autonomous driving technologies.
