<p><p>The optical communication equipment market encompasses three main types: modulator/demodulator, transmitter/receiver, and optical fiber. Modulators and demodulators convert electrical signals to optical signals and vice versa, enabling efficient data transmission. Transmitters and receivers facilitate the sending and receiving of these optical signals, ensuring reliable communication. Optical fibers are the physical medium through which light signals travel, offering high bandwidth and low attenuation. Together, these components form the backbone of modern optical communication systems, enhancing data transfer across networks.</p></p>
