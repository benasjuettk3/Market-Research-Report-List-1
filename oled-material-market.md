<p><strong>The OLED Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HTL/BTL/HBL</li><li>HIL</li><li>Encapsulation</li><li>EML</li><li>Substrate</li><li>Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED material market encompasses various components essential for display and lighting applications. HTL (Hole Transport Layer) enhances hole mobility, while BTL (Buffer Transport Layer) serves to improve charge balance. HBL (Hole Blocking Layer) prevents holes from reaching undesired layers. HIL (Hole Injection Layer) facilitates hole introduction into the emissive layer, which is termed EML (Emissive Layer) and responsible for light emission. Encapsulation protects the OLED from environmental factors, while substrates provide structural support, and anodes serve as electrical contacts.</p></p>
