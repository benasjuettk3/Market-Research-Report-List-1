<p><strong>The Insulin Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Pump</li><li>Patch Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulin pump market primarily features two types: normal pumps and patch pumps. Normal pumps are typically worn on the waistband or belt, delivering insulin through a connected catheter. They offer precise dosing and programmable features for personalized therapy. In contrast, patch pumps are a more discreet option, adhering directly to the skin and providing insulin via a built-in cannula. Their wireless design enhances convenience and mobility, appealing to users seeking a less obtrusive diabetes management solution.</p></p>
