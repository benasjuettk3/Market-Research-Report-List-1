<p><p>Solar roof mounts are categorized by the roof type they are installed on, including clay tile roofs, asphalt roofs, composite roofs, and shake & slate roofs. Clay tile roofs require specialized mounts that accommodate their unique shape and weight, while asphalt roofs utilize simpler, often less expensive mounting solutions. Composite roofs offer flexibility in mounting options due to their lightweight materials.
