<p><ul><li>Electrophotography</li><li>Inkjet printing</li><li>Thermal transfer printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital printing packaging using flexible plastic encompasses various technologies, including electrophotography, inkjet printing, and thermal transfer printing. Electrophotography uses electrostatic charges to transfer toner onto the substrate, creating high-quality images suitable for short runs. Inkjet printing employs droplets of ink ejected onto the material, allowing for vibrant colors and intricate designs, ideal for customized packaging. Thermal transfer printing utilizes heat to transfer ink from a ribbon onto surfaces, ensuring durability and resistance, commonly used for labels and barcodes in packaging applications.</p></p>
