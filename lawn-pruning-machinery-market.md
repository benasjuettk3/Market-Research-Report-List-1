<p><p>The lawn pruning machinery market comprises three main types. Rotary lawn mowers, the most commonly used, feature a rotating blade and are suitable for various grass types and terrains, making them versatile for larger areas. Scissors lawn mowers, similar to hobbing mowers, provide an eco-friendly option with manual operation, appealing to those seeking sustainable gardening practices.</p></p>
